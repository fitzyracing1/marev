Optional: Claim Window

If set, recipients can only claim before this date. After expiry the creator can call sweep() on the distributor to recover unclaimed tokens. Leave empty for no expiry.

Launch

Launch will create the token, deploy a fresh airdrop distributor, fund it, and publish your claim data. You'll confirm 3 transactions in MetaMask. Total cost: launch fee (above) + ~$0.50 in Base gas.
