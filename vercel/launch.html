Ship a Base ERC-20
with a built-in airdrop.

Create the token, deploy a Merkle distributor, fund it, publish a claim link — in one flow. Recipients claim themselves with one transaction.

1
Connect wallet
Connect Wallet on Base. Need ~0.001 ETH for the launch fee plus a tiny bit of gas.
2
Define your token
Pick a name, ticker symbol, decimals (usually 18), and total supply.
3
Add recipients
Paste one wallet per line as address,amount. They'll be eligible to claim their share.
4
Launch + share
Hit Launch, confirm 3 wallet popups, get a shareable claim URL. Recipients claim themselves.

2// TOKEN DETAILS

Standard ERC-20 contract. The full supply mints to your wallet first; later, the airdrop allocation is transferred from your wallet to the distributor automatically. Decimals is almost always 18 — leave it unless you know why you'd change it.

3// AIRDROP RECIPIENTS

Who gets free tokens. Format: address,amount — one per line. Amount is in human units, not wei. 500 means 500 tokens (the contract handles decimals). Lines starting with # are comments. Click Validate Recipients to compute the Merkle root before launching.

Tip: click Load Sample List first to see a working example with your wallet on it. Edit it to your real recipients, then click Validate Recipients to compute the Merkle root.

4// CLAIM WINDOW (optional)

How long recipients have to claim. After this window passes, you (the creator) can call sweep() on the distributor to recover unclaimed tokens. Leave blank if you want claims to stay open forever.

5// LAUNCH

When you click below, you'll see 3 wallet popups in sequence:
  1. Deploy your ERC-20 token (you pay the launch fee).
  2. Deploy a Merkle distributor for the airdrop.
  3. Transfer the airdrop allocation from your wallet into the distributor.
Then we publish your Merkle data and give you a public claim URL to share. Total cost: launch fee (above) + about $0.50 of Base gas. Stay on the page until you see the green result box.
